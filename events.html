4×4 Dance Championship

The Final Saturday 13th July 2019 Arena Square Way HA9 0AA Free

4x4 Dance Championship

An event produced by Impact Dance in collaboration with The Yellow, Wembley Park

4 x 4 Dance Championships is an exciting dance competition created by Impact Dance. Young people between the ages of 11-19 who have a passion for dance have formed a TEAM of 4 MEMBERS.

This is the first of two heats which will see young people from across London competing for a cash prize. The top 2 in each category from 2 heats have progressed to the Grand Final. At this final each group will have the chance to perform one piece relating to an undisclosed theme. The top group in each category will then perform their wildcard piece.

There will only be ONE winner in each Catergory.

The final will be judged by a team of professional judges.

The overall winners of each category will win a massive £500.00 per team!

Come down and support this talented bunch!
